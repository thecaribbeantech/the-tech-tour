OUR CONVICTION

Because we believe caribbean people are innovators who can change the world, we want to let everybody knows about this through a study which will be published in early 2015.

For this reason, our Paris-located team will fly to the West-Indies to meet the caribbean startup ecosystem in order to get authentic testimonies from entrepreneurs, investors, accelerators, incubators and officials.

Before coming back in Paris to share conclusions, we want to go and meet american actors to understand their main tips in creating big and useful companies.
